# Phase 7: Launch Plan & Post-Launch

## Purpose
Orchestrate the launch timeline and plan for what happens after.

---

## Launch Timeline Template

```markdown
# LAUNCH TIMELINE: [Course Name]

## Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)
- **Week -6:** Finalize course content, start beta testing
- **Week -5:** Build sales page, set up tech stack
- **Week -4:** Launch lead magnet, start growing waitlist
- **Week -3:** Send teaser content to email list / social
- **Week -2:** Open early bird / waitlist signups
- **Week -1:** Final email sequence loaded, everything tested

## Launch Week
- **Day -1:** Final teaser email + social post
- **Day 0 (LAUNCH):** Announcement email + social post + sales page live
- **Day 1:** Deep dive email (what's inside)
- **Day 3:** Social proof email (testimonials/results)
- **Day 5:** Objection killer email (FAQ)
- **Day 7:** CART CLOSE — urgency email + social post

## Post-Launch (immediately after)
- **Day 8:** Welcome enrolled students, kickoff call
- **Day 9+:** Deliver course content per schedule
- **Ongoing:** Monitor engagement, respond to community, gather feedback

## Post-Course (after course completes)
- **Week +1:** Student survey (feedback collection)
- **Week +2:** Testimonial collection
- **Week +3:** Course iteration based on feedback
- **Week +4:** Plan for next cohort / evergreen conversion
```

Customize all dates based on the user's actual launch date.

---

## Beta Testing Strategy

```markdown
# BETA TEST PLAN

## Who
5-15 people from your target audience

## Price
Free or heavily discounted (50-80% off)

## What They Get
Full course access + direct line to instructor

## What You Get
Detailed feedback + testimonials + case studies

## Feedback Collection
- Daily/weekly check-in survey (3-5 questions)
- Post-course survey (comprehensive)
- 1:1 interview with 3-5 beta students
- Permission to use their results as testimonials

## Key Questions
1. Was the pacing too fast, too slow, or just right?
2. Which lesson was the most valuable? Least valuable?
3. Was anything confusing or unclear?
4. What was missing that you wished was included?
5. Would you recommend this course? Why / why not?
6. What result did you achieve?
```

---

## Post-Launch Feedback Loop

```markdown
# POST-LAUNCH OPTIMIZATION

## Metrics to Track
- Enrollment numbers
- Completion rate (per module and overall)
- Drop-off points (which lesson do people stop at?)
- Student satisfaction (NPS score)
- Testimonial quality
- Revenue / ROI

## Feedback Mechanisms
- End-of-module micro-surveys (2-3 questions)
- End-of-course comprehensive survey
- Community sentiment monitoring
- Support ticket patterns

## Iteration Cycle
After each cohort/launch:
1. Review all feedback
2. Identify top 3 improvements
3. Implement changes
4. Test with next cohort
5. Repeat
```

---

## Scaling & Evergreen Strategy

```markdown
# SCALING OPTIONS

1. **RUN AGAIN:** Launch next cohort (apply learnings)
2. **EVERGREEN:** Convert to self-paced with automated delivery
3. **UPSELL:** Create advanced/next-level course
4. **DOWNSELL:** Create a lighter/cheaper intro course
5. **BUNDLE:** Package with other products/courses
6. **LICENSE:** Allow others to teach your course
7. **AFFILIATE:** Set up affiliate program for student referrals
```
