# Phase 5: Sales & Marketing Assets

## Purpose
Create everything needed to sell the course — sales page, email sequences, social posts, pricing strategy.

---

## Sales Page Copy (12-Section Framework)

```markdown
# SALES PAGE: [Course Name]

## Section 1: Hero
- **Headline:** Transformation promise (8-12 words)
- **Subheadline:** How they'll achieve it (15-25 words)
- **Hero image/video:** Instructor or course mockup
- **Primary CTA:** "Join the [Course Name]" or "Start Building Today"

## Section 2: Pain Points (The Problem)
- "Does this sound familiar?" / "Are you tired of..."
- 3-5 specific pain points in the student's own language
- End with: "It's not your fault — here's why..."

## Section 3: The Bridge (Why Existing Solutions Fail)
- What they've probably already tried
- Why those approaches didn't work
- The missing piece they haven't found yet

## Section 4: The Solution (Your Course)
- Introduce the course as the answer
- Unique positioning angle
- The transformation they'll experience
- "Introducing [Course Name]: [tagline]"

## Section 5: What's Inside (Curriculum Overview)
- Module-by-module breakdown
- For each module: title + 2-3 bullet points of OUTCOMES (not just topics)

## Section 6: Who This Is For / Not For
- "This course is perfect for you if..." (3-5 qualifying statements)
- "This is NOT for you if..." (2-3 disqualifying statements)

## Section 7: Social Proof
- Student testimonials (3-5 minimum)
- Results/statistics
- Logos/media mentions (if applicable)

## Section 8: Instructor Bio
- Personal story (relatable origin)
- Credentials/experience
- Why they created this course
- Photo

## Section 9: Bonuses (if applicable)
- 2-3 bonuses that enhance the core offer
- Each with: name, description, value
- "Total value: $X,XXX — yours for just $XXX"

## Section 10: Pricing
- Price clearly displayed
- Payment options (one-time, payment plan)
- What's included at each tier (if tiered)
- Money-back guarantee details
- CTA button

## Section 11: FAQ
- 8-12 questions addressing objections:
  - "I don't have time"
  - "I'm not technical enough"
  - "Is this worth the investment?"
  - "What if I fall behind?"
  - "How is this different from [competitor]?"
  - "What if I want a refund?"

## Section 12: Final CTA
- Urgency element (limited spots, deadline, bonus expiry)
- Restate transformation
- Final CTA button
- "Still have questions? [Contact link]"
```

## Launch Email Sequence (7 Emails)

| # | Email | Timing | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | The Teaser | 7 days before | Warm up, start conversation |
| 2 | The Pain | 5 days before | Agitate the problem |
| 3 | The Reveal | 3 days before / launch day | Announce the course |
| 4 | The Deep Dive | Launch + 1 day | Walk through curriculum |
| 5 | The Proof | Launch + 3 days | Testimonials, results |
| 6 | The Objection Killer | Launch + 5 days | FAQ, address barriers |
| 7 | The Close | Final day / cart closing | Urgency, last chance |

For each email, write:
- Subject line (with alternatives)
- Full email body copy
- CTA (call-to-action)
- P.S. line

## Lead Magnet

Design a free resource that feeds into the course:

```markdown
# LEAD MAGNET BRIEF

- **Title:** [Name of free resource]
- **Format:** [PDF guide / mini-course / webinar / checklist / template]
- **Topic:** [Directly related to course topic]
- **Transformation:** [Small, quick win that makes them want more]
- **Call-to-action:** [Leads into course enrollment]
- **Delivery:** [Email opt-in → immediate access]
```

**Example:**
> "5 Claude Projects You Can Build in Under 10 Minutes" — A free PDF with 5 copy-paste prompts that produce impressive results. CTA: "Want to build 30 more? Join the challenge."

## Social Media Posts (5 Posts)

| # | Post Type | Timing | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Teaser | 1 week before | Story/insight, no course mention, ends with question |
| 2 | Announcement | Launch day | What it is, who it's for, transformation, CTA |
| 3 | Behind the Scenes | Launch + 2 days | Course creation process or lesson preview |
| 4 | Social Proof | Launch + 4 days | Testimonial or student result |
| 5 | Last Call | Cart closing day | Urgency, deadline, what they'll miss |

Write full copy for each post, ready to publish.

## Pricing Strategy

Provide a recommendation based on:

| Factor | Input |
|---|---|
| Course format | Self-paced / Cohort / Challenge / Hybrid |
| Transformation value | What's it worth to the student? |
| Support level | Self-guided / Group / 1:1 |
| Market comparables | What do similar courses charge? |
| Audience budget | What can your target student afford? |

```markdown
# PRICING RECOMMENDATION

**Recommended Price:** $[X]
**Payment Plan:** [X] payments of $[Y]

## Justification
- Transformation value: $[estimated value to student]
- Market range for similar courses: $[X] - $[Y]
- Format premium/discount: [explanation]
- Support level premium: [explanation]

## Tiered Pricing (if applicable)
- **Tier 1 (Self-paced):** $[X] — Course content only
- **Tier 2 (Standard):** $[Y] — Content + community + live Q&A
- **Tier 3 (Premium):** $[Z] — Everything + 1:1 coaching calls

## Launch Pricing
- **Early bird:** $[X] (valid until [date])
- **Regular:** $[Y] (after launch)
```
